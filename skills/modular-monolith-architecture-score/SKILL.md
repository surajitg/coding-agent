# Modular Monolith Architecture Score

## Purpose

Evaluate quality of modular monolith architecture.

Score range:

0 – 100

---

# Scoring Criteria

Module encapsulation: 30

Modules should hide internal implementation.

---

Module independence: 25

Modules should communicate through interfaces.

---

Dependency management: 25

Avoid circular dependencies between modules.

---

Domain boundary clarity: 20

Modules should represent business capabilities.

---

# Score Calculation

Architecture Score =

Module Encapsulation  
+ Module Independence  
+ Dependency Management  
+ Domain Boundary Clarity

Maximum Score: 100

---

# Output

Architecture Score: X / 100

Strengths  
Weaknesses  
Risk Level
